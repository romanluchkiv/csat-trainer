## CSAT Training Simulator

You're about to go through **4 customer support cases** in sequence. Each case is based on a real ticket from our Zendesk and uses an AI to play the role of the customer.

### What to expect

- 4 cases, one after another. Each takes ~10 minutes.
- Reply to the customer like you would in Zendesk: public replies and internal notes (to Ops or IT).
- The AI customer reacts to your tone, decisions, and pace.
- Your booking information is shown on the right side of the screen.

### Rules

- **Reply in your own words.** Macros from Zendesk are fine. AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, copilot) are **not**.
- **Stay in character.** Treat each case as if a real customer is on the other side.
- **Take your time.** There is no countdown for the manager pilot.
- **Don't worry about score between cases** — you'll see all results only at the end.

### Internal notes routing

- Start internal notes with **"Dear Ops"** for refund/operator/carrier issues.
- Start internal notes with **"Dear IT"** for payment errors, "Something went wrong" page errors, or platform glitches.
